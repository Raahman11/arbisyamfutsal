About Us

Arbisyam Futsal Club founded in 2005 and affiliated to the first season of the Brunei Futsal League. Arbisyam FC never won the league, however, Arbisyam FC managed to be in the top 3 position throughout the history of the Brunei Futsal League. The latest success in the hunt for the first title was in 2018 where the club were unbeaten throughout the campaign but lost by a point to Kuantan Rangers from Malaysia.

The Grow Sportswear company from Indonesia has been the team's main sponsor since the 2018 season, the year in which the Arbisyam FC was selected to represent Brunei Darussalam for the AFF Futsal Club Championship.

BFL 2017 campaign

BFL 2017 Campaign

The year of the unbeaten campaign for Arbisyam FC end in a sad second place behind Kuantan Rangers. The final match of the Brunei Futsal League was held at the Brunei Darussalam Indoor Stadium. The night was filled with thunderous atmosphere as the local club Arbisyam FC is going head to head against Kuantan Rangers from Malaysia. Both teams were unbeaten thoughout the campaign. In the of the match it as only a draw. Both teams had their chances but failed to take the lead against one another. As the final whistle blown, Kuantan Rangers celebrates the draw and crowned as Champion for 2017 Brunei Futsal League. It is clear that with the help and influence of the World class player like Rico Dzulkarnain, a Welsh national, they manage to be unbeaten in the league and make Kuantan Rangers a team to be scared off. As the trophies being presented to the Champion, medals tot he second runner up, the best players of the league was given to Mohd Nabil, the goalkeeper of Arbisyam FC for his outstanding performances.

Winning the Weekend Warrior Tournament

Weekend Warrior Tournament Winner

ULocal startup Weekend Warriors have teamed up with Red Bull Malaysia to host a futsal competition called the Red Bull Warriors Cup and Championship 2018 which begins this Sunday, July 1 at International School Brunei.

The Red Bull Warriors Cup and Championship is the energy drink’s first major futsal championship in Brunei, and will eventually be launched regionally using the Weekend Warrior’s gamified platform that tracks individual player performance and rewards them with points and prizes.

The competition will run in the standard team tournament style on Sunday – beginning with group stages followed by knockout rounds where 20 teams are targeted to compete with cash prizes of $1,000 for the winning team. The championship continues over the next four weekends, where individual players from the participating teams are able to win prizes across the four categories of top scorer, top assist, top player, and top goalkeeper.
