About Us

Arbisyam Futsal Club founded in 2005 and affiliated to the first season of the Brunei Futsal League. Arbisyam FC never won the league, however, Arbisyam FC managed to be in the top 3 position throughout the history of the Brunei Futsal League. The latest success in the hunt for the first title was in 2018 where the club were unbeaten throughout the campaign but lost by a point to Kuantan Rangers from Malaysia.

The Grow Sportswear company from Indonesia has been the team's main sponsor since the 2018 season, the year in which the Arbisyam FC was selected to represent Brunei Darussalam for the AFF Futsal Club Championship.
